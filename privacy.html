What data we collect and why

Contact form and email

When you use the intake form on the Book page, the data you enter (name, organisation, email, service, notes) is sent directly to our email address via your own email client. We do not store this data on any server. We use it solely to respond to your enquiry and, if you become a customer, to deliver the agreed service.

Legal basis: Legitimate interest (pre-contractual steps) and, where an engagement follows, performance of a contract (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)).

Analytics cookies (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics 4 with Consent Mode v2. Analytics cookies are only placed after you explicitly accept via the cookie banner. If you decline, no analytics cookies are set and no data is sent to Google.

Legal basis: Consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)).

Calendly

The Book page embeds a Calendly scheduling widget. If you interact with it, Calendly's own privacy policy applies. We have no access to data you enter in Calendly beyond what Calendly sends us as a calendar notification.

Data sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except:

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, to restrict or object to processing, and to data portability. Contact us at hans.vredevoort@cldlbs.com to exercise these rights.
